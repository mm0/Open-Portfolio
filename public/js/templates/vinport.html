Vinport.com

Vinport.com was launched in 2012 as a marketplace for limited deals on various wines from around the globe.

Vinport seeked to incorporate a model similar to daily deals site as well as a custom rewards platform for encouraging customers to become repeat customers and earn more and more discounts and rewards for their interaction on the site as well as their purchases.

As lead developer, it was my goal to setup a storefront that would be seamlessly integrated with custom shipping and management solutions and develop custom features and extensions on top of Magento CE.

Implementation of custom extensions including customization of SweetTooth’s Reward platform was involved. I Implemented designs in CSS3 and customized Magento templates, Back-End code and features. In addition to this, system administrator duties were my responsibility.
