Wondermode.com

wonderMode.com was founded in 2011 seeking to disrupt the high-end fashion market by creating an online platform for introducing fresh, up-and-coming designers from around the globe.

As lead developer at wonderMode, it quickly became evident that a custom platform was required to be able to manage thousands of designers and their products while seeking to push the boundaries of e-Commerce. This platform was setup to allow for maximum customization with minimal effort by administrators.

Multiple custom CMS systems were integrated including: A user interface for designers to manage product uploads and custom brand pages including lookbooks and a separate storefront. An e-commerce platform to manage products available for sale and orders. A custom interface to allow Administrators to approve designers, their products, generate invoices and track transactions.

I developed the Front-End User Interface and Back-End code. I was also in charge of administration of Apache and Linux servers. Prominent features of this project include: order and payment processing, shipping automation, and various API integrations and content management.
